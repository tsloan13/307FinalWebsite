About Our Team

We are a passionate and dedicated group of women who share a love for water polo, team sports, and building a strong, supportive community. Our team is open to players of all skill levels, from beginners looking to learn the game to seasoned athletes seeking competitive play. What unites us is our commitment to growth, teamwork, and having fun in and out of the pool.

We strive to create an inclusive and empowering environment where every player feels valued, challenged, and supported. Whether it’s through rigorous practice sessions, friendly competition, or social events, we work hard to build both individual skills and team chemistry. Our goal is to create an experience where women can thrive, make lasting friendships, and push themselves to achieve more.

Water polo is more than just a sport; it’s about discipline, teamwork, and resilience. We are proud to represent our team, our values, and each other—whether we’re competing in tournaments or cheering each other on from the sidelines. Join us in making waves and growing stronger, together. We can’t wait to see what we can achieve as a team!

Alumni

As part of the JMU Club Water Polo family, you’ve been an integral part of our rich history and tradition. Whether you competed in thrilling matches, pushed your limits in training, or built lifelong friendships, your contributions have shaped the legacy we continue to uphold today.

This page is a place for alumni to stay connected, celebrate achievements, and support the next generation of water polo athletes. We invite you to share updates, memories, and stories, as well as get involved in future events, fundraising initiatives, and mentoring opportunities. Your experiences continue to inspire our current team, and your involvement helps us build a stronger, more united community.

Thank you for your continued support. We look forward to hearing from you and seeing how you’re making waves in the world beyond the pool!

If you want to stay in touch about any alumni events, check out or alumni Facebook page.

Join

Whether you're a seasoned player or totally new to the sport, our women’s college club water polo team welcomes all skill levels. We're a competitive, fun, and supportive group that values teamwork, fitness, and friendship—both in and out of the pool. No tryouts, no experience required—just bring your enthusiasm and a willingness to learn. Come to a practice, meet the team, and see what we’re all about!

Come find us at Student Org Night! If you have questions, please feel free to email our President, Sarah, at jmuwomenswaterpolo@gmail.com

Service

At JMU Women’s Club Water Polo, we take pride in being active members of our community both in and out of the water. Our team is dedicated to service initiatives that promote inclusion, wellness, and support for those in need. Every year, we help set up the Pride Parade to stand in solidarity with our LGBTQ+ community, partnered with Empowerment3 to encourage physical activity and well-being, helped combat food insecurity through the Harrisonburg Food Drive, and brought energy (and pancakes!) to Late Night Breakfast. These experiences allow us to grow as athletes, leaders, and citizens—and we’re proud to give back to the community that lifts us up.
